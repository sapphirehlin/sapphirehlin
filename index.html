Profile

Sapphire H. Lin

Sapphire Lin is presently a Research Fellow at the Institute for Digital Medicine (WisDM), Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine, National University of Singapore, and the N.1 Institute for Health, National University of Singapore. Beyond academia, Sapphire has worked in healthcare research at SingHealth, Singapore's largest public healthcare cluster.

Sapphire holds a doctoral degree in Communication from the Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, as well as a Master of Mass Communication from the same institution.

Her research interests center on mobile communication, particularly in health applications, focusing on the impact of social ties and health technology on older adults' health and well-being.
